Dear Abby,

My penmanship isn't great like yours, so I hope this will do.
I can do my best to fit my love for you into sentences, but I can't really make up words to express how I feel for you.

and it hasn't been one second that I have not felt your love and constant support for me in everything I do.
For that, I'll always be grateful.
You've been the biggest light in my life since I met you and my only regret is not finding you sooner.
Hopefully these small things will show you a little bit of how much I really love you.

Always yours. Love,
Ant
